# Zeichenketten und Zahlen

```typescript
let appleAverageWeight = 0.19;
let applesPricePerkg = 3.99;

let applesPerKg = 1 / appleAverageWeight;

console.log("20kg apples cost: " + applesPricePerkg * 17);
console.log("With 20kg apples you get about " + applesPerKg * 20 + " apples");
```
